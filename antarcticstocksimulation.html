What is Antarctic Stock Simulation?

An in-development stock exchange game. This game has 2 components: single player and multiplayer. The single player version operates purely as a game, run locally on a computer without needing an Internet connection. In this game, multiple agents invest alongside you influencing Antarctic Stock Simulation to behave as a complex system, like the New York Stock Exchange.

The multiplayer version is planned as a stock exchange that operates without borders to sell shares of companies in The Matrix. Players concurrently play in this Bloomberg-like terminal. All listed companies have been vetted by The Matrix. Listing criteria are changing the world, and The Matrix's ownership. The multiplayer stock exchange is for mathematical minds to game.

To play in multiplayer, you need to own at least 1 share of Antarctic Stock Simulation. The $1000 we collect helps us operate the game. Single player will be released for free.
