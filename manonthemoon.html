MAN ON THE MOON


1 year on the Moon

This corporation builds cheap accessible Moon travel. Projects include space station hotels, Moon bases, interplanetary trade, space communication networks and others that bring civilization on the Moon.

This company uses Wetwood Housing, CNS robots and ASBL. It aims to bring (metaphorical) man on the Moon by putting one man (founder) and his army of bots on the Moon. The task is to prepare the Moon for civilization. Moonflower, in the vein of the Mayflower.
